Crux - The Reader's Avenue !!

The official newsletter of Math Computing Society, CRUX-The Readers Avenue, is one of many elements that rises the society's bar high. Providing an acumen to its readers and bringing out a concrete impact, the newsletter intends to bring out the voice of the students of Department of Mathematics. This alluring 8 pages magazine is an annual edition that looks forward to exclusively cover the diverse happenings in the field of Computers and Information technology through articles on interesting technology, geek-humours, thereby making its fellow bookmen, aware of the world outside the college gates! Addingly, it punctuates the masses with the events happening around the department, the most supportive and best alumni ever and their talks and various other innovative ideas penned down in a lucid manner that entices the students to read. Every edition tries its hand on bringing out something new to its readers and covering all domains that a reader shall expect from a newsletter and we work on accomplishing them with fullest efforts. Needless to say, Crux is an added feather on the cap of Math Computing Society!
