The Math Computing Society is a student run society started by M.Sc Integrated(5 years) students of CEG, Anna University in the year 2010. The society has been moulding each and every student to par with the industries' expectation. By conducting several technical events such as workshops, seminars, programming contests and non-technical events such as biz quiz, group discussions to name a few, the society keeps the momentum going throughout the academic year.
